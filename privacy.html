Privacy Policy for Gmail Storage Analyzer

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. The app runs entirely in your browser. Gmail data is accessed via Google's API and never leaves your device.

Data Usage

Email metadata (sender, size, date) is analyzed locally in your browser to generate storage visualizations. No data is sent to any servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we never receive your data in the first place.
